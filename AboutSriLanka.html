Sri Lanka, officially known as the Democratic Socialist Republic of Sri Lanka, is an island country located in South Asia. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and diverse landscapes. The country has a long and storied history, with evidence of human settlement dating back to the prehistoric era. Today, Sri Lanka is a popular tourist destination, attracting millions of visitors each year with its stunning beaches, lush forests, and ancient ruins. The country is also known for its rich cuisine, which is a fusion of Indian, Chinese, and European influences.
